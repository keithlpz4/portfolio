Griffin's first Day of School

Author: Jason Ruby

Publisher: Future Publishing House

Griffin's First Day of School tells the story about a young griffin learning to the creature of myth and legend. Young reader follow the young creature as he learns vaulable life lessons of conquering your fears and stick up for others, even if they were mean to you.

This project was hand-drawn by me and colored by author Jason Ruby.
