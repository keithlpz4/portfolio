About Me

My name is Keith Lopez

I’m a designer in the Baltimore/ Washington DC area. I am a requirements-driven, user-centric web designer.

What does this mean exactly?

My years as an Information Systems Professional has allowed me to focus on the key components to ensure the success of each project and help identify problems that users may encounter. This process involved collaborating with various stakeholders on the goals of the project, designing user and process workflows, and project monitoring for continual improvements.

Why switch to design?

Perhaps the biggest frustration among users with IT systems has been when it looks cool it doesn’t work or if it works it was not well designed. As a creative at heart, I decided to marry my IT knowledge and experience to help better design user-centric systems that meet the system’s functional requirements.

P.S. I’m also a cartoonist with a published children’s book: Griffin’s First Day of School. It is available online on Amazon, Target or other online retail bookstores.
