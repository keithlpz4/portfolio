About Me

My name is Keith Lopez

I’m a designer in the Baltimore/ Washington DC area. I am a requirements-driven, user-centric web designer.

What does this mean exactly?
My years as an Information system professional has allowed me to focus on the key components to ensure the success of each project and help identify problems that users may encounter. This process involved collaborating with various stakeholders on the goals of the project, design user and process workflows, and project monitoring for continual improvements.

Why switch to design?
Perhaps the biggest frustration among users with IT systems have been when it looks cool it doesn’t work or if it works it was not well designed. As a creative at heart, I decided to marry my IT knowledge and experience to help better design user-centric systems that meets the systems functional requirements.

P.S. I’m also a cartoonist with a published children’s book: Griffin’s First Day available on Amazon, Target and other online retail bookstores.
